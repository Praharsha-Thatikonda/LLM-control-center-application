Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resolve CUDA Out of Memory errors?
Try reducing the batch size in your training configuration, or enable 4-bit quantization (QLoRA) to significantly lower VRAM usage. Gradient Checkpointing is also recommended.
Can I import custom GGUF models?
Yes! Navigate to the Model Registry > Import tab, and either drag-and-drop your .gguf file or provide a local path to ingest it into the system.
Where are my checkpoints saved?
Checkpoints are stored in the /data/checkpoints/ directory relative to your installation. You can view and manage them directly from the Training > Checkpoints tab.
How do I connect to an external Ollama instance?
Go to Settings > Integrations. Enter your Ollama host URL (e.g., http://localhost:11434). The system will auto-detect available models.
